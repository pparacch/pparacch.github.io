I’m Pier Lorenzo Paracchini, a "generalist" with a passion for people, data and technology. I am Italian and, currently, living in Stavanger (Norway).

I have a Master of Science in Electronic Engineering from the “Politecnico Di Milano”, a valuable experience in Software Engineering and, at the moment, working as an enthusiast developer with a data scientist twist in the Software Innovation sector in Statoil.

I am intrigued by data science and machine learning. My journey in data science and machine learning has started in 2014, when for curiosity I started the first module of the "Data Science Specialization" at Coursera. Since then I have been hooked within those domains.

I have learnt a lot of things, and still, have many exciting and interesting things to learn and master. In this site I will capture the interesting experiences, learning and experiments found on my journey.
