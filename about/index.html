I’m Pier Lorenzo Paracchini, "generalist" with a passion for people, data and technology.

I am Italian and, currently, living in Stavanger (Norway). I have a Master of Science in Electronic Engineering from the “Politecnico Di Milano”, a valuable experience in Software Engineering and, at the moment, working as an enthusiast developer with a data scientist twist in the Software Innovation sector in Statoil.

I am intrigued by data science and machine learning. My journey into data science and machine learning has started in 2014, when for curiosity I started the first module of the "Data Science Specialization" at Coursera. Since then I have been hooked with data science and machine learning.

I have learnt a lot of things, and still, have many exciting and interesting things to learn and master. In this blog I will capture the interesting experiences, learning I have found and I will find on my journey.
